This Week's Work: Data Collection

Your Task

You have been assigned 7-8 municipalities each. For these municipalities, you will collect data for the financial years ranging from 2013/14 to 2023/24 (where available).

A Note for Engineers

As student engineers, you are encouraged to problem-solve and apply yourselves. This program is an opportunity to use your analytical skills to tackle real-world challenges in municipal finance and infrastructure.

Data Collection Toolkit

You will primarily use National Treasury's official platforms. The most reliable data comes from Audited Financial Statements (AFS).

1. Municipal Money Website Good for overviews and calculated ratios 2. AFS Archive (Pre 2020/21) Direct access to historical audited statements 3. GoMuni Portal (Post 2020/21) For recent audited statements

The Importance of Audited Data

We are specifically using audited financial statements because they provide the most reliable and verified data. This process is important for ensuring the integrity and accuracy of your assessments, making your findings credible and actionable.
